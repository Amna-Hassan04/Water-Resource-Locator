Water Challenges in Rawalpindi: Addressing the Community's Needs

01

Water Scarcity

Rawalpindi faces significant water scarcity, especially during dry seasons. The growing population and inadequate water infrastructure contribute to an insufficient supply of clean and safe water for residential and commercial use.

02

Contaminated Water

Many areas in Rawalpindi struggle with water contamination issues. The presence of pollutants, bacteria, and other harmful substances in the water supply leads to health risks and waterborne diseases. 

03

Irregular Water Supply

The residents of Rawalpindi often experience irregular water supply, with frequent interruptions and low water pressure. This inconsistency poses challenges for daily activities, such as cooking, cleaning, and meeting basic hygiene needs.

04

Lack of Awareness and Information

There is often a lack of awareness among the residents regarding the availability, accessibility, and characteristics of water resources in Rawalpindi. This hampers their ability to make informed decisions about water usage and conservation.
